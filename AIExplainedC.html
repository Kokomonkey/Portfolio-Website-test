Architectural Diffusion

01. Input Generation

Task: Produce a conceptual volume study of a building structure.

Constraint: 60 Seconds.

The LLM requires a visual anchor. High fidelity is not required; accurate massing and perspective are prioritized to guide the ControlNet.

[IMG: Rough 60-second pencil/marker sketch of a tower block]
02. Prompt Engineering

"Act as a specialist in high-fidelity architectural visualization. Transform the provided sketch into a mixed-use tower located at Museumplein, Amsterdam, featuring a double-skin façade of glazing and timber lattice, crowned with honed basalt stone. Ensure the atmosphere reflects golden hour lighting conditions, 4K photorealistic resolution, and active pedestrian density."

03. Logic Decoding
  • System Instruction (Cyan) Defines the "Persona." This limits the model's latent space to professional architectural terminology, preventing generic or cartoonish outputs.
  • Contextual Anchoring (Magenta) Providing geographic data ("Amsterdam") and materiality forces the model to access specific training data regarding Dutch light, urban density, and texture.
  • Stylistic Tokenization (Yellow) "Golden Hour" and "4K" are high-weight tokens. They determine the noise-reduction path, ensuring the lighting and sharpness align with industry standards.
