About the Hub

Who Am I

I am an architecture student focusing on how to implemet AI in the everyday architectural process. My work focuses on how algorithms can augment the productive & creative process, moving beyond simple automation to genuine co-creation. Locations of study: - TU Delft 2024 - current [Architecture Bachelor] - NUS Singapore 2025 [non-faculty based Exchange] (but focused on architecture)

This is my first proper website and it is meant to both display my capabilities and to be a source of information for other architects (students).

What is this Webpage

This hub serves as a living documentation of my AI knowledge. It is not just a portfolio, but a deconstruction of my workflow used to generate architectural concepts and even more infomration on what it possible.

Here, I break down the specific AI's available, give some starter tips and tricks, and post-processing techniques used to achieve the best result. It is designed to be transparent.
